CopyCat.js — Interactive Demo

CopyCat.js motto: small · secure-first · dependable. Prefer modern navigator.clipboard.writeText, fall back to a safe DOM-based copy, provide feedback and easy hooks for success/error handling.

Demonstrates simple text copy and optional built-in feedback toast.
